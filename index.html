Simple.

"Keep it Simple, Stupid." This phrase was said to be coined by a navy officer decades ago. But the priciple has been true for as long as problems existed. When looking for an answer to a problem, not many people go looking for the prettiest or most complicated solution. It's usually the simple solution that wins.
